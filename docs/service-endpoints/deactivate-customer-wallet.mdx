---
title: "Deactivate Customer Wallet"
description: "This endpoint is utilized immediately for deactivating customer wallet"
api: "POST /api/provider/wallets/deactivate"
contentType: "application/json"
---

<Tip>
  For Test environment please use any random 6 digit Number. On Production,
  Customer would recieve a 6 digit OTP
</Tip>
