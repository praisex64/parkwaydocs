---
title: "Linking Customer Card to Customer Wallet Account"
description: "This endpoint is to enable customer card to be linked to the customer Wallet account per partner institutions"
api: "POST /api/provider/wallets/{walletAccountNumber}/linkcard"
---

## Path Parameters
<ParamField path="walletAccountNumber" type="string" required>
  Customer Wallet Account Number to link to Card
</ParamField>

## Request Parameters
<ParamField body="lastFour" type="string" required>
  Last Four Digits of Card
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="requestReference" type="string" required>
  Last Four Digits of Card
</ParamField>
