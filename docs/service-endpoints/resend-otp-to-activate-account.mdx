---
title: "Resend OTP to activate Customers Wallet"
description: "If for any reason Customer did not recieve OTP through SMS to activate Wallet within five(5) minutes. This endpoint can be used trigger a new OTP SMS"
api: "POST /api/provider/wallets/{accountNumber}/otp"
---
<Note>Ensure Customers interactions with this endpoint is delayed at least for about 5 Minutes as users sometimes may not get the SMS on-time due to several factors. This will help to prevent customers from receiving too many tokens and could get confused about the right one.</Note>

<Tip>This endpoint can also be used to verify if a Wallet has been activated.</Tip>


## Path Parameters

<ParamField path="accountNumber" type="string" required>
  Customer Wallet Account Number
</ParamField>
